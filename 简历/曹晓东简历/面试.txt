Then, I added composite indexes on `user_id` and `verify_time` to speed up user-specific log queries. Additionally, I used Redis to cache hot data (e.g., verification results of the last 24 hours). These optimizations reduced query time for historical logs from 3s to 200ms and supported stable operation during peak traffic.
